5 This chapter establishes a framework to support the integration of information security in the services provided by government information processing facilities.
5 Planning and management of the day-to-day activities is required to ensure the availability and capacity of the resources that provide services.
5 Services can be delivered by external parties and by computer networks and by all services that exchange information.

5 Controls for operations include documented processes, staff duties and formal methods to implement changes to facilities.
5 This includes: methods to protect information, create copies for back-up and to manage the media where those copies are stored.
5 Network protection requirements from threats such as viruses or unauthorized disclosure are also described.
